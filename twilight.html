What is a Twilight Photo (Day to Dusk)?

A twilight photo is taken usually of the exterior of the property, at dusk. It is used to showcase landscape/property lighting, pool lighting, and features like fire pits, and also to showcase a beautiful sunset.

Agents like twilight photos because they “look cool”, and they are different from the regular exterior shot. They are more likely to grab the attention of the buyer when a home search is performed and thumbnails of properties are returned.

Benefits of a Twilight Photo:

They showcase property lighting that a buyer generally doesn’t see because they most likely are viewing the home in daylight.

They allow you to stick out from your competition by offering a service they don’t offer.

They allow you to add a high-value service to your product list.

They bring clients to you who normally list higher-end properties, and are more likely to use twilight photography services.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Twilight photography? keyboard_arrow_down

Twilight photography is a technique where photos are taken during the "magic hour"—the period just after sunset or just before sunrise. The goal is to capture the warm, soft glow of the sky and the lights of the property, giving the image a stunning and inviting atmosphere.

Will Twilight photography make my property look unrealistic? keyboard_arrow_down

No, our Twilight photography aims to create an enhanced yet realistic image. We focus on capturing the natural beauty of the property during twilight while ensuring that the final image reflects a balanced and accurate representation of the property.

How long does it take to receive the final Twilight photos? keyboard_arrow_down

Our goal is to deliver your edited Twilight photos within 48 hours after the shoot. We take great care in editing to ensure the final images meet your expectations.

Do I need to schedule a special time for Twilight photography? keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, Twilight photography requires shooting at specific times—just before sunset or just after sunset, when the lighting is optimal. We’ll help you find a convenient time and make all necessary arrangements.

Is Twilight photography suitable for properties with large outdoor spaces? keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, Twilight photography works wonderfully for properties with expansive outdoor spaces, as it highlights landscaping, pools, and other exterior features in a captivating way. The lighting during this time can create a magical effect for large areas.

Are there additional costs for Twilight photography? keyboard_arrow_down

Twilight photography typically involves a special scheduling process and may require additional time and resources. For exact pricing details, please refer to our pricing page or contact us directly for a quote based on your specific needs.

Can Twilight photography be done indoors as well? keyboard_arrow_down

While Twilight photography primarily focuses on the exterior of the property, we can certainly incorporate interior shots if your space has large windows that allow the outside ambiance to blend with the indoor lighting. Our photographers will be able to create a seamless transition from the exterior to the interior for a cohesive look.
