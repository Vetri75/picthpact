What is Virtual Renovation?

Virtual Renovation is a cutting-edge service that digitally redesigns and updates spaces to showcase their maximum potential. By enhancing property images with modern designs, updated finishes, and creative layouts, virtual renovation helps homeowners, agents, and developers reimagine a property’s true possibilities.

Whether it's revamping interiors, upgrading kitchens, or giving facades a fresh look, Virtual Renovation brings bold transformations to life — all without lifting a single tool.

Why Virtual Renovation Matters:

Highlight the Possibilities: Buyers can visualize the future of a space, helping them connect emotionally with the property.

Cost-Effective Marketing: Save on expensive physical renovations by digitally presenting a renovated version.

Attract More Buyers: Create standout listings that captivate buyers with modern, fresh designs tailored to market trends.

Increase Property Value: Showcasing a renovated aesthetic can boost perceived value and shorten the time on the market.

How Virtual Renovation Works:

1. Submit high-resolution images of your property. Clear and detailed photos help us create accurate virtual renovations.

2. Share your design preferences, including styles, colors, and specific features. This ensures the transformation aligns with your vision.

3. Receive realistic, transformed images showcasing your updated space. Visualize the changes before moving forward with any decisions.

4. Request adjustments if needed. We’re happy to refine the design to make sure it perfectly matches your expectations.

The Advantages of Choosing Us:

Our team specializes in creating hyper-realistic renovations that align with buyer expectations.

We ensure every image is accurate, natural-looking, and aesthetically pleasing.

Designs are tailored to fit the property’s architecture and your goals, ensuring no detail is overlooked.

Poorly executed virtual renovations can look fake or misleading, pushing buyers away. We focus on crafting professional, high-quality transformations that inspire trust and interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really get my virtual renovation in just 1 week? keyboard_arrow_down

We have a global team of gurus working 24 hours, 7 days a week to deliver your virtual renovations on time!

Provided you supply all the required information when you submit your job, you can receive your virtual renovation back within 5-7 business days.

If your job is flagged and we need to receive more information from you or revisions are required, it may take longer to complete your job.

What information do I need to provide to complete a virtual renovation? keyboard_arrow_down

The more information supplied the better! At minimum, we need:

1. A high resolution image.

2. A detailed style/design brief including build changes, fixture changes, furniture description, textures, color scheme, etc.

3. Reference images or mark-up sketches.

What if I want to change something? keyboard_arrow_down

Please note that Virtual Renovation is a “first and final” output. You will be required to provide a full design brief before commencement of the virtual renovation project.

Small changes that fall within the original design brief and are requested within 2 months of the original job, will be made at no cost. If you change your mind from the original design brief, additional fees will be charged.

Do you offer volume discounts? keyboard_arrow_down

Sorry, there are no volume discounts available. We operate on high volume and low margins and offer the same low price to all customers.

How long do you store my images for? keyboard_arrow_down

After we finish your edits, we will store them for a minimum of 90 days. To ensure you have a lasting copy, kindly download and save the files to your own system.
